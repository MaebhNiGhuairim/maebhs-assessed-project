Explore Our Yoga Classes

Hatha Yoga
Hatha Yoga focuses on foundational poses, breathing techniques, and relaxation. It's perfect for beginners looking to build strength and flexibility.
Vinyasa Yoga
Vinyasa Yoga emphasizes flowing sequences, linking breath with movement. Ideal for those who enjoy dynamic and creative yoga practices.
Ashtanga Yoga
Ashtanga Yoga is a structured and powerful practice that follows a set sequence of poses. Great for disciplined yogis looking to deepen their practice.
Yin Yoga
Yin Yoga is a slow-paced style focused on long-held poses to target deep connective tissues. Perfect for relaxation and mindfulness.
